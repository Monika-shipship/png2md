## H原子模型的检验

### Pickering series
$n = 5, 6, 7, \ldots \to m = 4$

$$\tilde{\nu} = 2^2 R_\infty \left( \frac{1}{m^2} - \frac{1}{n^2} \right) = R_\infty \left( \frac{1}{(m/2)^2} - \frac{1}{(n/2)^2} \right)$$

→ $$\tilde{\nu} = R_\infty \left( \frac{1}{2^2} - \frac{1}{k^2} \right)$$

$k = n/2 = 2.5, 3, 3.5, 4, \ldots$

### 光谱观测
$$\tilde{\nu} = R' \left( \frac{1}{2^2} - \frac{1}{k^2} \right)$$

$R' = R_{He^+} = 1.097,222,7 \times 10^7 \text{ m}^{-1}$
