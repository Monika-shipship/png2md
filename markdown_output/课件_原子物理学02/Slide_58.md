# H原子光谱和Bohr原子模型

## H原子的定态能量

$$ r_n = \frac{4\pi\varepsilon_0\hbar^2}{m_e e^2} n^2 \quad \text{代入} \quad E = -\frac{1}{2} \frac{e^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r} $$

得
$$
E_n = -\frac{1}{2} \frac{e^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r_n} = -\frac{1}{2} \frac{e^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0} \frac{m_e e^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 \hbar^2 n^2} = -\frac{1}{(4\pi\varepsilon_0)^2} \frac{m_e e^4}{2\hbar^2 n^2}
$$
$$
= -hcR_\infty \frac{1}{n^2} \quad n=1,2,3,\dots
$$

### 里德伯常数的理论公式
$$ R_\infty = \frac{1}{(4\pi\varepsilon_0)^2} \frac{m_e e^4}{4\pi\hbar^3 c} $$

$$ E_n = -\frac{1}{2} m_e \alpha^2 c^2 \frac{1}{n^2} $$
