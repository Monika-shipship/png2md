## 原子模型：α粒子散射实验和卢瑟福原子模型

$ctg \frac{\theta}{2} = \frac{2b}{D}$ → $db = -\frac{D}{4} \frac{1}{\sin^2 \frac{\theta}{2}} d\theta$

所以 $d\sigma = 2\pi b |db| = \frac{\pi D^2}{4} \frac{\cos(\theta/2)}{\sin^3(\theta/2)} d\theta$

又 $d\Omega = \frac{S}{r^2} = \frac{2\pi r \sin\theta \cdot r d\theta}{r^2} = 2\pi \sin\theta d\theta = 4\pi \sin\frac{\theta}{2}\cos\frac{\theta}{2}$

得 $d\sigma = \frac{D^2}{16} \frac{1}{\sin^4 \theta/2} d\Omega$
