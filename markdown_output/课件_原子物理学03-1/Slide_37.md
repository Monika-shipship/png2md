# 实物粒子的波动性–不确定关系

1927年，海森堡提出不确定关系

$$
\left\{
\begin{array}{c}
\Delta p_x \Delta x \ge \frac{\hbar}{2} \\
\Delta p_y \Delta y \ge \frac{\hbar}{2} \\
\Delta p_z \Delta z \ge \frac{\hbar}{2}
\end{array}
\right.
\quad \Delta E \Delta t \ge \frac{\hbar}{2}
$$

Werner Heisenberg (1901-1976)

It is not possible to know the value of all the properties of the system at the same time; those properties that are not known with precision must be described by probabilities.
